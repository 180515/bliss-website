About our retreats

We provide exclusive service to make sure young women get the opportunity to experience a week of retreats in order to find their true inner self. The women who’ll join us have to hand in their phone and will get them back for the last three days. We’ll use experts for this to teach things like yoga and meditation. Also speeches will be given by someone who knows all about insecurities and how to gain confidence. These women will come back stronger, empowered and confident. For people who are shorter in cash or don’t have a whole week because of work or other factors, we also provide retreat weekends. On the weekends, you don’t get to use your phone at all.
